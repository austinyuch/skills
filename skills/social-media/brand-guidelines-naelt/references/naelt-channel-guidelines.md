## Media/Public Relations

**Status:** PROVISIONAL

### Press Releases

**Format Guidelines:**
- Letterhead with full logo
- Contact information at top
- "For Immediate Release" dateline
- Headline: Clear, under 120 characters
- Subheadline: Supporting detail (optional)
- Dateline: City, Date
- Body: Inverted pyramid structure
- Boilerplate: Organization description at end
- End notation: ###

**Tone:** Professional, factual, newsworthy

### Media Interviews

**Spokesperson Guidelines:**
- Clear, quotable statements
- Bridge to key messages
- Factual accuracy essential
- Empathy without overpromising

## Cross-Channel Consistency

### Elements to Maintain Across Channels

1. **Logo Usage:** Consistent lockup and placement
2. **Color Palette:** Primary red, secondary gray, accent orange
3. **Typography:** Noto Sans TC primary, Noto Serif TC for weight
4. **Tone:** Professional, empathetic, advocacy-oriented
5. **Messaging:** Alignment with core appeals and values

### Channel-Specific Adaptations

| Element | Website | Social | Email | Print |
|---------|---------|--------|-------|-------|
| Logo | Full lockup | Simplified | Full lockup | Full lockup |
| Colors | Full scale | Optimized for screens | Full scale | CMYK (needed) |
| Typography | Web fonts | System fonts | Web-safe fonts | Print fonts |
| Layout | Responsive | Platform-native | Fixed width | Fixed layout |
